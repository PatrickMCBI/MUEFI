Chinese New Year 2026
Announcement

Posted: January 23, 2026

Dear Parents/Guardians,

We hope this letter finds you and your family well as we gear up for the vibrant celebrations of Chinese New Year 2026! Our annual presentation is a cherished tradition that brings our students together to showcase Chinese culture through dance, music and traditional attire.

To ensure every child shines in authentic costumes that reflect the spirit of the occasion, we are preparing custom outfits for all participants.

For payment information, please check your GC for further details.

Thank you for your unwavering support in nurturing our students' love for Chinese Traditions. We look forward to a spectacular performance!

Note: Boys & Girls foot wear must be Black Shoes.
