Explore our easy-to-follow admission process for students from Preschool to Grade 12.

Admission Requirements
  • Completed Admission Form
  • Birth Certificate
  • Report Card or School Records
  • Recent Photo of the Student
  • Medical Records (Preschool – Optional)
Admission Policy

Our school follows a non-discriminatory policy, welcoming students from all backgrounds. Admission is based on age eligibility, assessment results, and readiness for the appropriate grade level. We prioritize holistic development and maintain a safe, inclusive environment for all learners.

Enrollment Guide
  1. Submit the completed application form and required documents.
  2. Attend the student assessment or interview (if required).
  3. Receive admission confirmation from the school.
  4. Complete enrollment by submitting remaining documents and paying tuition fees.
  5. Attend orientation day before classes start.
Payment Methods
  • Bank Transfer (Online or Branch)
  • Credit/Debit Card Payment
  • Cash Payment at the School Office
  • Installment plans available for selected programs
Pre-Registration

Parents may pre-register their child online or at the school office. Pre-registration allows early placement and priority scheduling for assessment and orientation. Spaces are limited, so early application is recommended.
